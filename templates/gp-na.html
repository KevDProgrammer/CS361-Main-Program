NieR:Automata

Set in a desolate world in the year 11,945 AD, NieR:Automata features the long-standing war between the Machine Lifeforms and the Androids, humanity's last hope. As 2B and 9S traverse the post-apocalyptic wasteland where human society once reign and thrived, their missions and duty as YoRHa soldiers led to discoveries and the uncovering of forbidden truths.

The truth of humanity and the values of life. Do these grand purposes hold the same weight for Androids and Machines? For the moment being, the annihilation of the wretched machinery is all that matters.
