NieR Reincarnation

The spin-off to the Nier/Drakengard series, this mysterious narrative-driven game features rich independent stories, accompanied by beautiful soundtracks, as well as additional lore to other existing titles.

Awaken from a deep slumber, where the beginning is nigh but forgotten, a young girl finds herself in the cell of a tower, where with each wandering attempt, paths strech endlessly before her. Who are they? What is this place? Why are they here? Their memory is but an empty shell, but perhaps the paths forward may reveal some answers.

(Expect turn-based battles and a friendly gacha system)
